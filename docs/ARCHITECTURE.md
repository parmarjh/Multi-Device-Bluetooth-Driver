# System Architecture

## Multi-Device Bluetooth Driver with AI Integration

---

## Overview

The Multi-Device Bluetooth Driver system is designed as a cross-platform solution that enables simultaneous connections to multiple Bluetooth devices (up to 7+) with AI-powered optimization. The architecture consists of platform-specific components (Windows and Android) that share common AI models and protocols.
